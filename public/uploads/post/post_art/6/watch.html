The best Drone Videos ever from December/ January 2016 – Drone footage is awesome. The drone videos in this compilation were all filmed with drones such as the DJI Phantom 3, the DJI Inspire 1 and the DJI Phantom 2 Vision+. All the footage you see in this video was sent to us by the original creators, and the result is one of the best drone videos ever. If you would like to submit your drone video, find us here https://facebook.com/dronevideos

Let us know what you thought of this video in the comments!

Visit https://travelwithdrone.com to discover some amazing aerial videos from around the world!

This epic drone video compilation is jam packed with epic aerial footage. From drone videos of wakeboarding and downhill longboarding, there is drone footage over the Grand Canyon.
The fall colours in Sweden, and the islands in El Nido and Palawan. No drone video compilation would be complete without some epic drone footage of fireworks from the air, the clip in this drone video compilation has been slowed down to extra slow to capture the fireworks exploding in slow-motion.

Below is a list of the EPIC creators and drone pilots who helped make this compilation of the best drone footage of December and January 2016 possible.

No videos on our channel would exist without our epic cominity and subscribers, thank you everyone who is involved!
